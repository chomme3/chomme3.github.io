About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics in West Point's Department of Social Sciences, and I am a Research Analyst in the Army's Office of Economic and Manpower Analysis (OEMA). My primary research interests are labor, personnel, and health economics. I am currently a Major in the United States Army.
